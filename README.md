# 🛩️ Flybax

> A game about cheating on exams and helping your classmates — one paper ball at a time.

Flybax is a Unity-based game where you throw paper balls across the classroom to help your fellow students. Master your aim, control your throw force, and don't let the teacher catch you.

---

##  Description

You're stuck in class, your classmate needs help, and the only solution is a perfectly aimed paper airplane. Flybax challenges your precision and timing in a classroom setting where every throw counts. Adjust your force, line up your shot, and launch.

## 🕹️ Usage / Controls

| Input | Action |
|---|---|
| **Left Click** | Throw the paper ball |
| **W** | Increase throw force |
| **S** | Decrease throw force |
| **R** | Retry / reset the throw |

### Tips

- Hold **W** before throwing to build up more power for long-distance targets.
- Use **R** to cancel a bad throw angle and reposition.
- Aim carefully — you only have so many chances before the teacher notices.
